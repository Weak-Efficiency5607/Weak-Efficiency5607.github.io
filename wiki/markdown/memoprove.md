MemoProve is a dietary supplement designed to support cognitive function, particularly memory. Its primary active ingredient is N-PEP-12 (a patented blend of neuropeptides derived from purified nerve cell proteins, often from pig brain via enzymatic hydrolysis), at a dosage of 90 mg per tablet.

The excipients (inactive ingredients) typically include:

- Lactose
- Microcrystalline cellulose (E 460)
- Croscarmellose sodium (E 468)
- Cellulose acetate phthalate
- Triethyl citrate
- Magnesium stearate (E 572)
- Silicon dioxide (E 551)
- Talc (E 553b)
- Titanium dioxide (E 171)
- Synthetic paraffin

Note that formulations may vary slightly by region or manufacturer, but the supplement is generally free from psychostimulants, yeast, artificial additives, sugar, and common allergens like dairy. The standard dosage is one tablet per day.
