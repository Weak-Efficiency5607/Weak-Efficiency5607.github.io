LOINC Database

Logical Observation Identifiers Names and Codes (LOINC) is the international standard for identifying health measurements, observations, and documents.

🇨🇦 Note for Canada: The official Canadian standard for lab tests, pCLOCD, is directly based on LOINC. You can use this search to find Canadian health tests.

🇪🇸 Note for Spain: To solve regional fragmentation, the Spanish Ministry of Health (SNS) adopted LOINC as the official exchange standard. Select the "Spanish (SPAIN)" linguistic variant below to find official Spanish laboratory codes.
